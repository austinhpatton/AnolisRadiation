This directory holds data used in the publication. 

Anole-TimeCal_NoOut.tree - this is the time-calibrated phylogeny without outgroups used in all analyses - obtained from Poe et al. 2020 - Systematic Biology. 
Anole-No-Secondary-Island-Col.tree - this is the same as above, but with species that secondarily colonized islands from the mainland removed. 
Patton-et-al-2021-PNAS-FINAL-DATA.csv - the full dataset, with morphological and ecological measurements, as well as information on each species' biogeographic distributions.
